Weixin Chen

Second-year PhD Student @ UIUC

I am a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Han Zhao.

Before that, I obtained my M.E. degree in Artificial Intelligence from Tsinghua University and B.S. degree in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University. I was a research intern at CUHK-Shenzhen (advised by Prof. Baoyuan Wu) and at UIUC (advised by Prof. Bo Li).

My research interests lie in trustworthy machine learning (interpretability, robustness, generalization) and trustworthy LLMs (truthfulness, reasoning).
